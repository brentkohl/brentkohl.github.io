Golf Caddie(WIP)

This is a project that acts as a golf caddie for golfers. Based off the mode selected, either it will choose the best club from your bag for the given distance and the percentage that it needs to be hit to match the distance given or use the tour average distance numbers to calculate those numbers. It will also take into account the shot type and if on the putting surface, the average percentage to make a putt from the given distance.
